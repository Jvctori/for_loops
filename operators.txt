Logical Operators (Operadores lógicos):

	! (logical NOT: não)
	|| (logical OR: ou)
	&& (logical AND: e)

Bitwise operators: 

	& (bitwise AND)
	| (bitwise OR)
	^ (biwise XOR)
	&^ (bitwise, AND NOT)
	<< (left shift)
	>> (right shift)

Comparison Operators (Operadores de comparação):
	== (equal: é igual a)
	!= (not equal: não é igual a)
	< (less than: menor que)
	<= (less than or equal to: menor ou igual que)
	> (greater than: maior que)
	>= (greater than or equal to: maior ou igual que)
